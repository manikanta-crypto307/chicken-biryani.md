Chicken Biryani

Ingredients:
- Rice
- Chicken
- Onion
- Tomato
- Biryani Masala
- Oil
- Salt

Steps:
1. Marinate the chicken with spices.
2. Cook the rice separately.
3. Fry onions and tomatoes.
4. Add chicken and cook well.
5. Mix cooked rice with chicken.
6. Cook on low flame for 15 minutes.
7. Serve hot.
